# Intentia Assurance — Procurement Summary

## What We Offer

Intentia Assurance provides two independent assurance records:

• **Training Assurance Pack** — documents what training was intended to do and what was delivered

• **Programme Decision Record** — documents why a programme was commissioned before outcomes exist
