## 1. Services

Intentia Assurance provides independent assurance records documenting information supplied and confirmed by the Client in relation to organisational training programmes.

## 2. Nature of Service

The service is documentary and procedural in nature. Intentia Assurance does not design, deliver, evaluate, assess, certify, or provide advice regarding training programmes or participants.

## 3. Client Responsibility

The Client is responsible for the accuracy and completeness of all information and materials supplied. Intentia Assurance relies on Client confirmation prior to record freeze.

## 4. Finality

Once a record is frozen and generated, no changes can be made. Corrections require a new record.

## 5. No Outcomes or Guarantees

The assurance record does not imply effectiveness, competence, capability, or accountability for outcomes.

## 6. Limitation of Liability

Intentia Assurance's liability is limited to the fees paid for the specific record.

This agreement is governed by the laws of England and Wales.
