**What This Record Does Not Claim:**

- This record does not assess or certify the effectiveness of the decision
- This record does not evaluate whether the decision was correct
- This record does not guarantee that the programme will achieve its intended purpose
- This record does not provide legal, HR, or organisational advice
- This record does not interpret incidents, grievances, or disputes
- This record does not assess outcomes or results (outcomes did not exist at decision time)
